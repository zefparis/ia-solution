Cookie Policy

Last updated: {{ today }}

1. What is a Cookie?

A cookie is a small text file placed on your device (computer, tablet, or mobile) when you visit our site. It allows us to store information related to your browsing and to offer you services adapted to your preferences.

2. Types of Cookies Used

Our site uses different types of cookies:

  • Essential cookies: necessary for the functioning of the site
  • Functional cookies: allow us to remember your preferences and choices
  • Analytical cookies: allow us to analyze the use of the site to improve it
  • Performance cookies: measure the performance of the site
  • Advertising cookies: allow us to display relevant advertisements

3. Cookie Retention Period

The lifespan of cookies varies according to their type:

  • Session cookies are temporary and expire when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies remain on your device until they expire or until you delete them

4. Cookie Management

You can choose to disable these cookies at any time. Your browser can also be set to notify you of cookies that are placed on your device and ask you whether to accept them or not.

To manage cookies, you can configure your browser:

  • For Chrome: https://support.google.com/chrome/answer/95647
  • For Firefox: https://support.mozilla.org/en-US/kb/cookies-information-websites-store-on-your-computer
  • For Safari: https://support.apple.com/guide/safari/manage-cookies-and-website-data-sfri11471/mac
  • For Edge: https://support.microsoft.com/en-us/microsoft-edge/
